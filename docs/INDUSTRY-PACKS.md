# Industry Packs

## Purpose
Industry packs provide tailored workflows, compliance controls, and UI presets while keeping the core platform shared.

## Pack Structure
- **Workflows**: rules, approvals, routing logic
- **Templates**: meeting summaries, action types, doc schemas
- **Compliance**: retention, access control, audit requirements
- **Dashboard Presets**: KPIs and alerts by industry

## Initial Packs
### Healthcare
- HIPAA-safe meeting workflows
- Clinical handoff summaries
- ePHI access controls and audit policies

### Financial Services
- Recordkeeping and supervision flows
- Audit-ready decision trails
- Retention and export controls

### Professional Services
- Matter-based organization
- Privilege-aware content handling
- Client-segregated access rules

## Expansion Candidates
- Manufacturing / Industrial
- Construction / Real Estate
- Life Sciences / FDA-regulated

## Pack Governance
- Packs are versioned with backward-compatible changes
- Core platform APIs remain stable across pack versions
